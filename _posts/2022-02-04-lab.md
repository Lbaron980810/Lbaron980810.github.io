# problems with jekyll:

{% raw %}
使用`{{`或`{%`符号时会导致以下错误：
> Liquid Exception: Liquid syntax error (line 140): Variable '{{0, 1}' was not properly terminated with regexp: /\}\}/ in *** files.

解决方法：
在段前添加\{% row %\}, 段后添加\{% endraw %\}
